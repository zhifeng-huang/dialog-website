Designed for daily use

A calm place for treatment records.

DiaLog keeps the core routine close: what happened today, what needs attention, and what can be shared later as a report. The examples below use simulated records to show the shape of the app without exposing real health information.

Treatment modes

Built around real dialysis routines.

Each treatment type has its own dashboard, log flow, history, reminders, and supporting tools. DiaLog is not a generic notes app wearing a medical label.

CAPD

Exchange-by-exchange tracking

Track daily CAPD exchanges, drain and fill details, fluid appearance, dialysate concentration, optional diabetes fields, daily summaries, and exit-site checks.

  • Missed and overdue exchange awareness
  • Exit-site notes and optional photos
  • Supply tracking and low-stock alerts
CCPD

Night setup and morning review

Record cycler setup, solution bags, pre-treatment values, morning UF and drain details, dwell changes, alarms, symptoms, and exit-site checks.

  • Cycler and prescription defaults
  • Solution bag tracking
  • Nightly supply monitoring
In-center HD

Treatment logs plus diary

Record in-center treatment status, weights, blood pressure, pulse, UF, symptoms, access concerns, and between-session diary notes.

  • Treatment day and chair-time reminders
  • Access detail and concern tracking
  • Between-session check-ins

What users notice first

Practical features, not noise.

The most important parts of DiaLog are the ones that reduce daily friction and make records easier to keep, protect, and share when the user chooses.

PDF

Care-team-ready reports

Create local PDF history reports from user-entered records, including treatment details and trends. Reports are shared only when the user chooses.

Bell

Optional reminders

Schedule local treatment reminders and optional medication reminders. Reminder schedules are convenience tools, not emergency or clinical decision systems.

Lock

Encrypted backup and restore

Create a password-protected backup file and save it wherever the user chooses. DiaLog cannot recover forgotten backup passwords.

Box

Supplies and low-stock awareness

CAPD and CCPD users can track important supplies and see low-stock status before routine materials run short.

Privacy first

Your records stay yours.

No account. No hidden sync.

DiaLog stores personal health information locally on the user's device. It does not require an account and does not upload treatment records to a DiaLog server.

Exports are user-controlled: PDF reports and encrypted backups leave DiaLog only when the user chooses to share or save them.

DiaLog does not:

  • Use ads
  • Use third-party analytics
  • Track users across apps or websites
  • Automatically share records with care teams
  • Use iCloud or CloudKit health-data sync

Safety note

A personal record, not medical advice.

DiaLog is a personal record-keeping tool. It does not diagnose, treat, monitor, or provide medical advice. Users should follow their care team's instructions and seek medical help immediately in an emergency.
